Services

Fabric Research

We have a library in our New York Office of China origin fabrics. Knit fabrics and woven fabrics. Work with the designers with fabrics. The design or development team can give a wishlist and we would prepare fabrics on that list.

Development

Making photo samples from information provided by the client. Info can be tech packs and or pattern and or original garments.

Pattern/Sample making

Our china office houses our pattern room, cutting room, and sample hands. We can produce photo samples and fit samples in-house.

Quality Production

Our own Quality Control team does cutting, inline, end of line and shipping inspections in the factories.

Shipping

We can handle all steps in the shipping process from china export documents through delivery to your D/C distribution center. We will customs clear and pay duties as part of our service.

Delivery

we can deliver to you anywhere in the US D/C or deliver to local port for your own trucks pickup.
